VISION

Our vision at DAV Public School, Haldia is to nurture the potential and embrace the uniqueness of each child. We endeavour to encourage students to become empathetic global citizens and practice the core values of the school.

MISSION

Our mission is to

• Spread the joy of learning through creativity, righteousness and valour.

• Value excellence by creating an inviting environment in which respect and values are balanced against academic rigour.

• Foster tolerance and inclusion through the spirit of camaraderie.

• Ensure that students develop competencies essential for success and leadership in the changing and complex global society.
